Our
Value Preposition

Simple, agile solutions

Short, sweet, painless, customer-centric solutions built to ensure minimum “remembering’ of details needed to make payments

Cognitive and intuitive solutions

Intelligent insights from structured and unstructured data. Detailed reporting and customized dashboards to suit your needs

Scalability

No infrastructure investment. Short delivery timelines. Deployment can be both local and cloud-based

Omni-channel solutions

Agnostic to customer touch points, can be deployed on SMS, USSD, app, web and social media.

Easy integrations

One integration exposes our clients and their customers to all payments methods. Integrations through APIs

Automated reconciliation

Integration to our clients’ systems allow us to reconcile all payments and customer engagement interactions
